There are various types of fonts available, including serif, sans-serif, monospace, and cursive. Each type has its own unique characteristics and is suitable for different purposes.

Web designers often use web-safe fonts to ensure that their text appears consistently across different browsers and devices. Additionally, custom fonts can be embedded using CSS to provide a unique look and feel.
