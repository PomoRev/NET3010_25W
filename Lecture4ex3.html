Iron golems are large, strong utility mobs found in the game Minecraft. They are created by players to protect villages from hostile mobs and can also be found naturally in villages. Iron golems are known for their immense strength and durability, making them formidable defenders. They are neutral towards players but will attack any hostile mob that threatens the village.
